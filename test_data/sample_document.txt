# Product Manual - TechPro 5000

## Installation Guide
The TechPro 5000 can be installed on Windows 10 or later. Download the installer from our website and run the setup.exe file. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete installation.

## System Requirements
- Windows 10/11 or macOS 10.15+
- 8GB RAM minimum (16GB recommended)
- 500MB available disk space
- USB 3.0 port required

## Battery Life
The Pro model battery lasts approximately 12 hours with normal usage. Heavy usage may reduce battery life to 6-8 hours.

## Warranty Information
All hardware defects are covered under our 2-year warranty from the date of purchase. This does not cover damage from misuse or accidents.

## Password Reset
If you forgot your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email.
